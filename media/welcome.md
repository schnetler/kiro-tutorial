## Why Spec-Driven Development?

- **Clear Documentation** - Everyone understands the project goals and decisions
- **Better Collaboration** - Works seamlessly with team members and AI agents
- **Track Progress** - See what's done and what's next
- **Reduce Ambiguity** - No more guessing what needs to be built
