### 1. Start with Requirements
Write down what you need to build in `requirements.md`. Get feedback and refine before moving forward.

### 2. Design Your Solution
Based on requirements, create your technical design in `design.md`. Consider alternatives and document your decisions.

### 3. Break Down Into Tasks
Convert your design into concrete tasks in `tasks.md`. Estimate time and identify dependencies.

### 4. Execute Tasks
Work through tasks one by one, checking them off as you complete them.

### 5. Update and Refine
As you learn new information or face challenges, **update your specs**. These are living documents!
